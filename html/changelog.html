31 May 2021

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Removed the ability to make the RIG Mounted Energy Gun.

17 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Javelin Gun, a rare and powerful new weapon. It can nail victims to walls, and electrocute them to a crisp
  • Significantly increased the rate that ammo drops appear in random loot. As more weapons are added its been getting harder and harder to find ammo for a gun you're carrying.
  • Fixed shotgun magazines being red

16 May 2021

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • The Asteroid Defense System airlock now works.

11 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Exploder's detonate-on-charge now works again
  • Lowered the chance for clumsy mobs using guns to shoot themselves.
  • Portable scrubbers can now be dragged, and many other large objects too.

10 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Slightly increased the cooldown times on spitter and puker ranged attacks.
  • Spitter and puker ranged attacks now have a small shared cooldown, preventing both shots from firing simultaneously.

08 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added our first Crew Objective, the Asteroid Defense System. Now fully functional, the ADS can break down, or be sabotaged. A repair mission must be mounted to fix it, but necromorphs will lie in wait.

18 April 2021

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Fixed the SO not having keycard authorization access.
  • Fixed the Services jobs (Botanist, Line Cook, Bartender) having the wrong colors on the Occupation screen in the Character Setup.
  • Fixed the Mining Department sharing the same color as the Supply Department on the Occupation screen in the Character Setup.
  • MDs starting Medical skill has been bumped up to Experienced.
  • The SMO and MD's starting Athletics skill has been bumped up to Basic.
  • The SMO's starting Medical and Anatomy skill has been bumped up to Master.
  • Surgeon's starting Anatomy skill has been downgraded to Experienced.
  • Surgeon's starting Medical skill has been bumped up to Experienced.
  • The Forensics skill has been capped at Trained for most of the crew except for Medical personnel, who have it capped as Experienced, and Security personnel.
  • The Cooking, Botany, and Medical skills have been capped at Experienced for roles who don't use those skills for their job.
  • The Anatomy skill has been capped at Trained for roles who don't use that skill for their job.

15 April 2021

REsident55 updated:

  • Interaction sounds for airlock controllers, bioballistic delivery systems, DNA analyzers, fax machines, file cabinets, freezers, heaters, lysis-isolation centrifuges, photocopiers, keycard authentication devices, newscasters, secure safes & seed extractors.
  • Vending machines now have a denied sound for no access or bad payments.
  • Fixed fire doors not playing any sounds due to faulty code.
  • Fixed vendors playing the purchase sound at different volumes depending on context.
  • Minor grammar fixes for some item names and descriptions, mainly tool mods.

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Fixed the ADS room and airlock not getting power.
  • Fixed pipe meters on the Aegis Colony being the wrong type.
  • Removed the Service department access requirement for freezer lockers.
  • Added a Condimaster to the Messhall Kitchen.
  • Slightly re-arranged the ADS room and added a few extra supplies.

10 April 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Asteroid Defense System, located on the top deck, above the bridge. It is only accessible via spacewalk along the spine of the ship. The ADS will automatically shoot down incoming asteroids, and it can also be manually controlled. There is currently no need for the manual control, but maybe someday soon it will start breaking down....
  • Majorly revamped meteors. They now come in varying sizes, speeds, and rotation speeds, and they explode when destroyed in flight. Meteors will now always approach on the top deck, but will hit a random deck of the ship when they make contact
  • Meteor events are now enabled and can occur randomly. Meteors will generally come from the front of the ship, and will be shot down by the ADS as long as it's working.
  • Added toggleable blast doors to all the windows on the bridge, and a switch to control them. The outer wall of the bridge can now be turned into a metal shell at a moment's notice.
  • All doors and airlocks now take less damage from explosions.
  • Added more hull plating to the front of the ship on several decks, allowing a few meteor hits to be sustained before hull breaches start to occur.
  • Blast doors are significantly buffed, especially against explosions and meteors.
